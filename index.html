關於我們 About Us

五十四年匠心傳承

54 Years of Craftsmanship

僑峯企業股份有限公司成立於1971年，五十四年來專注於美術用品、木製工藝品及塑膠文具的設計與製造。我們的工廠位於台北市南港區，配備現代化製造設備，為全球客戶提供優質的OEM/ODM服務。

Kiu Fong Enterprise Co., Ltd. was founded in 1971, specializing in the design and manufacturing of art supplies, wooden handicrafts and plastic stationery for 54 years. Our factory is located in Nangang District, Taipei City, equipped with modern manufacturing facilities to provide quality OEM/ODM services to global customers.

從調色盤到木製畫架，從雕刻工具到陶瓷工藝品，我們致力於將傳統工藝與現代設計完美結合，為世界各地的藝術愛好者和經銷商帶來優質產品。

From palettes to wooden easels, from carving tools to ceramic crafts, we are committed to perfectly combining traditional craftsmanship with modern design, bringing quality products to art enthusiasts and distributors worldwide.
