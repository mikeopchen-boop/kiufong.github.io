About Kiu Fong

A Legacy of Quality Since 1971

Kiu Fong Enterprise Co., Ltd. was established in 1971 in Taipei, Taiwan. With over five decades of experience, we have grown to become a trusted manufacturer and exporter of quality stationery, art supplies, and wooden home decor products.

Our factory in Nangang District, Taipei, is equipped with modern manufacturing facilities to produce plastic stationery, art materials, wooden crafts, and home decoration items. We pride ourselves on delivering products that combine functionality with aesthetic appeal.
